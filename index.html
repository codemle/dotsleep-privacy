🔒 DotSleep 不收集、不上传、不出售任何用户数据。

All sleep records are stored only on your device or in your personal iCloud account.

📱 数据存储

📱 Data Storage

您的睡眠记录、设置偏好和个人目标均存储在以下位置：

  • 设备本地：通过 SwiftData / SQLite 存储在您的 iPhone 上
  • iCloud（可选）：开通会员并启用 iCloud 同步后，数据通过 Apple CloudKit 同步到您的私人 iCloud 账户，仅您可访问

Your sleep records, settings, and personal goals are stored in:

  • On-device: Stored locally on your iPhone via SwiftData / SQLite
  • iCloud (optional): If you enable iCloud Sync as a Premium member, data syncs to your private iCloud account via Apple CloudKit — accessible only by you

❤️ Apple Health 数据

❤️ Apple Health Data

DotSleep 可申请读取 Apple 健康 App 中的睡眠数据，用于自动导入睡眠记录。

  • 仅在您明确授权后才会读取
  • 健康数据不会上传到任何服务器
  • 数据仅用于在 App 内展示，不作其他用途
  • 您可随时在「设置 → 隐私与安全性 → 健康」中撤销授权

DotSleep may request access to sleep data in the Apple Health app to automatically import sleep records.

  • Access is only requested with your explicit permission
  • Health data is never uploaded to any server
  • Data is used solely for display within the app
  • You can revoke access at any time in Settings → Privacy & Security → Health

🔗 第三方服务

🔗 Third-Party Services

DotSleep 不集成任何第三方数据分析、广告或追踪 SDK。

使用到的 Apple 系统框架：

  • StoreKit 2：处理 App 内购买，遵守 Apple 隐私准则
  • CloudKit：iCloud 同步（可选，会员专属），数据归属您的 Apple ID
  • HealthKit：读取睡眠数据（需授权）

DotSleep does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.

Apple system frameworks used:

  • StoreKit 2: Handles in-app purchases, subject to Apple's privacy guidelines
  • CloudKit: iCloud sync (optional, Premium only) — data belongs to your Apple ID
  • HealthKit: Reads sleep data (requires your authorization)
